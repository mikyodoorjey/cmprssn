Architecting organizations for the era of ubiquitous intelligence.

The technology is ready. Most organizations aren't. Today's agent deployments inherit the same latency, approval queues, political seams, data fragmentation and industrial-era design debt that defines most companies. We redesign teams and departments from first principles to enable agent-native operating models — guided by the question of what organizations are and should become as we move toward AGI.

Redesign

Rebuild work for human-agent teams.

We redesign work starting with workflows, roles and mandates, decision rights, and data structures. These compose into a company-wide, machine-readable shared brain that compounds knowledge and systemic coherence.

Deploy

We take a technology-agnostic approach to building and deploying agent fleets tailored to each client context. Track transformation via our Atlas frontend — agents and humans connected to the organization-wide shared brain.
